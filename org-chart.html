How this works in practice

Every coordinator works closely with the Main Festival Coordinator, but most roles also depend heavily on each other. The Marketing Coordinator needs content from the Vendor and Artist Coordinators. The Vendor Coordinator works with the Info Tables Coordinator on space layout. The Finance Coordinator tracks payments that the Main Coordinator processes. Knowing the whole picture makes everyone better at their own piece.
