About the Festival

What we're building together

🎵

Live Music

Regional and visiting performers showcasing the diversity of Latin musical traditions — salsa, cumbia, bachata, folkloric, and more.

🎨

Interactive Art

Installations and showcases that invite community participation and hands-on cultural exploration.

🛍

Craft Vendors

Handmade goods from Latin American and US Latino artisans — not resellers of mass-produced goods.

🍽

Traditional Cuisine

Authentic dishes from multiple regions that tell stories of culture and family tradition through food.

About Latin Vibe

Latin Vibe is a Pennsylvania 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2023. Its mission is Hispanic leadership development in State College and the surrounding region. The Happy Valley Latin Festival, running since 2020, is its flagship annual event.

New to the committee?

Where to start

Read the Roles page

Understand what every position involves before the role-assignment meeting. This helps you pick something realistic for your schedule.

Read the Full Manual section for your role

Once you have a role, go deep on that section. It explains what to do, when to do it, and who you'll need to coordinate with.

Note the coordination points

Almost every role requires working with at least two or three other coordinators. Knowing this early prevents surprises later.

Come to the first meeting ready to commit

The first committee meeting is specifically for assigning roles. Reading the manual first means you can make an informed, realistic commitment.
